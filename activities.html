ACTIVITIES

The House Structure

When a student is admitted to the school, they are placed in one of four Houses: Amboseli, Boni, Wagalla or Elewak. There are regular inter-house cultural events, along with inter-house sports fixtures. Students in each House are appointed as House Captains and these provide positive opportunities for developing leadership skills.

The Arts

Major annual drama and musical productions are staged each year, ensuring that students have ongoing opportunities to exercise their talents in theatre, song and dance. Inter-house competitions also provide students with avenues to acquire the confidence to perform on stage.

Sports

Sport is an integral feature of life at Bidii high, allowing our pupils to experience a diverse range of sports to develop their mental, physical and social well being. We aim to ensure that students enjoy physical activity and that they can compete healthily in individual and team competition. Key to our approach is that students are taught the importance of good sportsmanship.

Music

Bidii high school nurtures a culture of music appreciation and participation in our students from an early age and students are given the opportunity to acquire music skills throughout the Prep and Secondary schools. In addition, the BTEC Diploma in Music offers an advanced foundation for entry to university or proceeding to employment in the music industry.
