Bidii School is secondary school started in 1985 to offer education. It was founded by Mr. Umutu A. Patel as a private school funded by tuition revenue, private donations and endowment income. The school is located in Kalulu estate, 2 km from Baidabo town, in Isiolo county. The school is reputed for its spiritual background and excellent performance in K.C.S.E exams. It is regarded highly in the county.
